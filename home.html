About Us

Our cattery specializes in Scottish Fold/Straight and British Shorthair cats. We are located in Columbus, Ohio. Golden Whiskers was established in early 2020 with lots of passion, love, and dedication. We're excited to start our journey of helping you unite with your new friend! We raise kittens in a friendly, safe, and comfortable environment that helps them bring out the best traits of their characters.

Healthy = Happy

Golden Whiskers is a TICA registered cattery. We take pride in continuing to procreate the purest, healthiest Champion bloodlines of Scottish Fold/Straight and British Shorthair cats. Following breeding guidelines is extremely important to us to ensure kittens' perfect health.

A Furever Friend

Besides looking gorgeous and cute, these breeds also carry the sweetest and most caring personalities. There is nothing m ore they like better than being around their people, participating in every activity. Scottish Fold/Straight and British Shorthair are famous for being affectionate, playful, and active pets.
